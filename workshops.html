Workshop — Practical AI

AI is not
going away.
Let's learn it
together.

A hands-on workshop designed to demystify artificial intelligence — no technical background required. Walk away with real skills, real tools, and the confidence to use AI in your work and life.

Format 2 Hours
Group Size Up to 20 participants
Level Beginner — No coding required
Location In-person or virtual

// What We Will Cover

Six topics.
All practical.

01

What AI Actually Is

We'll trace AI's history from the 1950s to today and dive into how modern models actually work under the hood. Understand what large language models are — what they can do, what they can't, and why that distinction matters.

02

The AI Landscape: Models & Players

A full map of the companies and models shaping AI right now — OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, Meta, and more. Closed vs. open-source, what each is built for, and how to choose the right tool for the job.

03

AI for Your Work

From writing and research to analysis and communication — how to integrate AI into your actual workflow to save time and sharpen your output.

04

Automation Without Code

You don't need to be an engineer to automate. We'll cover no-code tools that let you build smart, repeatable workflows around your everyday tasks.

05

Agentic AI & Vibe Coding

Stop asking AI questions — start assigning it tasks. We'll cover the shift from chatbots to agents, introduce vibe coding, and you'll build something real from scratch using nothing but plain English.

06

Ethics, Bias & Agency

AI has blind spots — and understanding them is a form of power. We'll discuss bias, data privacy, and how to stay in the driver's seat as these tools evolve.

// What We Will Do

Hands-on from
minute one.

01

Live Demos Together

We'll open the tools and use them in real time. You'll see exactly how to navigate each platform, what to type, and how to iterate when the first result isn't quite right.

02

Hands-On Exercises

You'll work through structured exercises designed around real scenarios — drafting, researching, summarizing, brainstorming. Each exercise builds a skill you can apply immediately after the workshop.

03

Build Something of Your Own

By the end of the session, you'll have created at least one AI-powered tool or workflow tailored to your own work — something you can take back and actually use on Monday morning.

04

Open Q&A & Discussion

Space to ask the questions you've been hesitant to ask elsewhere. No question is too basic. The goal is for everyone to leave feeling clear and confident — not overwhelmed.

05

Take-Home Resource Pack

Every participant leaves with a curated resource pack — tool guides, a recommended reading list, and a personal action plan to keep building momentum after the workshop.

// Who Is This For

Built for anyone who is
curious, not technical.

This workshop was designed for people who know AI is important but don't know where to start — or who have started and hit a wall. No engineering background. No prior AI experience. Just a willingness to engage.

It's a collaborative, direct space where the jargon gets explained. You're allowed to ask what things mean. That's the point.

Become Iron Man: AI literacy is increasingly a professional and personal advantage — and the gap between those who have it and those who don't is widening fast. This workshop exists to make you feel empowered to use AI in all facets of your life to be smarter, quicker and more insightful.

  • Founders and entrepreneurs building or growing a business
  • Professionals looking to stay competitive in an AI-driven workplace
  • Career changers exploring new directions with AI as a tool
  • Executives and managers who need to lead AI-literate teams
  • Creatives — writers, designers, strategists — incorporating AI into their practice
  • Academics and educators integrating AI into their teaching or research
  • Anyone who has felt left out of the AI conversation and is ready to change that
